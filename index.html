About the project

This project, presented by Quadri Olajide, visualises data from the World Happiness Report (2015 to 2019), which ranks 149 countries based on various factors that contribute to happiness, such as gdp and life expectancy. Through these visualisations, we will achieve the following:
• Explore happiness, GDP, and life expectancy scores across the World.
• Explore the relationship between GDP or life expectancy and Happiness scores.
• Investigate how happiness, GDP, and life expectancy scores have evolved over time for the top and bottom ranked countries.
• Discover the rankings of the different continents on the above mentioned indices.
